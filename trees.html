The Problem

In Loudoun County, trees are affected and damaged due to invasive species, pests, and deforestation. See how each cause is devastating to plants and the earth.

Why Trees are Important

Trees are used to make more than 5000 products, including chocolate, paper, and pencils. Trees provide oxygen for us and inhale carbon dioxide. Trees also help support the entire ecosystem.

Invasive Species and Pests

Invasive species such as aphids and emerald ash
borer destroy forests all over Virginia.

Deforestation

More than 10 acres of land in Loudoun County to be deforested. Deforestation also indirectly causes climate change, as more CO2 is allowed in the air.

Conclusion

In our community, trees are important and we
need to save them.
